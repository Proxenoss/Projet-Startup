Ice Cream and Environnement artisanal ice creams are a new kind of delights made with the greatest care and respect for nature

Succumb to I.C.E ice cream, in the form of garnished cones handmade with delicacy in our stores, our tastes are elaborate on site to obtain creamy and tasty textures. Our filled cones offer a new ice cream experience which allows a generous tasting until the end. Likewise, its ice creams respect authentic flavors and pronounced tastes that we wish to convey to you and which in our opinion is an obligation when creating an ice cream. So the three flavors on sale will delight the taste buds of many of you while waiting for the release of new flavors. All in one approach of simplicity and ecology with respectful products and a principle of eco-responsibility.

Let yourself be tempted by our creations and taste excellence

The story begins with a bored huff during a tasting after the scoop of ice cream is finished but the cone is still whole. Following this, the idea was born of an ice cream which is delicious at the beginning but also succulent at the end. It is therefore in Nantes in 2022 that Lechat Baptiste and Fokou Steve, decided to launch their brand of I.C.E ice cream with garnished cones in order to satisfy the most discerning taste buds. With a beginning complicated due to the lack of flavors offered, the brand is starting to gain momentum and attract seekers of originality. This will allow the creation of new perfumes.
